Bluetooth Information
====================

* Bluetooth is a wireless technology standard for exchanging data over short distances.
* using short-wavelength radio transmissions in the ISM band from 2400â€“2480 MHz. 
* Created by telecom vendor Ericsson in 1994.
* Bluetooth was standardized as IEEE 802.15.1.
* Bluetooth is a packet-based protocol with a master-slave structure. One master may
  communicate with up to 7 slaves in a piconet; all devices share the master's clock.
